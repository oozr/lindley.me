Welcome to CEFR.AI

This website builds on the work done by the Global Scale of English (GSE) and the Common European Framework of Reference (CEFR). We are not affiliated with either, but seek ways to apply the tools and promote their utility using artificial intelligence.

We two advanced text analysers: the GSE analyser for English and the CEFR analyser for other languages.

For more information on the GSE, the CEFR, and the formulas used in the analysers, head over to our blog.

Text Analysers

Our website offers two text analysers: the GSE analyser for English, and the CEFR analyser for Arabic, English, German, Italian and Spanish.

GSE Analyser

The GSE analyser is the most advanced English text analyser on the internet, being the only one to take into account the GSE 35,000 wordlist in order to give exact GSE scores.

For other languages please use the CEFR anlayser.

CEFR Analyser

Each CEFR language analyser draws upon academic research done into the unique aspects of the individual languages.
