Global Scale of English

English is the most widely spoken language in the world. It is the official language of 53 countries and spoken by over 1.5 billion people worldwide. The Global Scale of English (GSE) is a framework that measures English language proficiency on a scale of 10-90, with 10 being beginner level and 90 being expert level.

Importance of English

The importance of English cannot be overstated. It is the language of international business, education, science, and technology. Proficiency in English is essential for anyone who wants to succeed in the global economy.
